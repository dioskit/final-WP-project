Space Odyssey: Exploring the Cosmos Itinerary

Popular Destinations in Our Solar System

Mercury: The Swift Planet

Activities:

  • Observation of Mercury's surface and features
  • Study of Mercury's unique geological characteristics
  • Exploration of Mercury's extreme temperatures and lack of atmosphere

Highlights:

  • Witness the closest planet to the Sun up close
  • Marvel at the stark beauty of Mercury's desolate landscape

Venus: The Morning Star

Activities:

  • Study of Venus' thick atmosphere and runaway greenhouse effect
  • Observation of Venus' volcanic surface features
  • Analysis of Venus' extreme surface temperatures and pressure

Highlights:

  • Experience the beauty of Venus' brilliant appearance in the morning sky
  • Explore the mysteries of Venus' inhospitable environment

Outer Planetary Systems Exploration

Jupiter: The Giant Planet

Activities:

  • Observation of Jupiter's colorful cloud bands and Great Red Spot
  • Study of Jupiter's magnetic field and intense radiation belts
  • Exploration of Jupiter's diverse system of moons, including Europa and Ganymede

Highlights:

  • Marvel at the largest planet in our solar system
  • Discover the mysteries of Jupiter's turbulent atmosphere

Saturn: The Ringed Planet

Activities:

  • Observation of Saturn's stunning ring system and numerous moons
  • Study of Saturn's unique hexagonal cloud pattern at its north pole
  • Exploration of Saturn's magnetic field and auroras

Highlights:

  • Witness the breathtaking beauty of Saturn's rings up close
  • Explore the enchanting moons of Saturn, including Titan and Enceladus
